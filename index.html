How It Works

Join the revolution! Follow these simple steps to recycle plastic, earn rewards, and contribute to a sustainable future. 🌍💎

Step 1: Collect Plastic Waste ♻️

Gather plastic bottles, bags, and other waste materials and get them ready for recycling.

Step 2: Deposit at Collection Points 🏪

Find the nearest recycling center or partner drop-off location to deposit your collected plastic.

Step 3: Scan & Verify 📲

Use the PlastiRewards app to scan the QR code on your deposit receipt to confirm your contribution.

Step 4: Earn PlastiTokens 💰

Receive PlastiTokens in your Web3 wallet as a reward for your recycling efforts.

Step 5: Spend or Trade 🚀

Use your PlastiTokens for utility bills, airtime, shopping, or even trade them on our marketplace.
