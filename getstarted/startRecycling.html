Start Recycling with PlastiRewards

Join our mission to reduce plastic waste and earn rewards!

How to Recycle and Earn

1

Collect Accepted Plastics

Gather clean and dry plastic waste that is accepted by our program. See the list of accepted plastics below.

  • PET (#1) - Water bottles, soda bottles
  • HDPE (#2) - Milk jugs, detergent bottles
  • PP (#5) - Yogurt containers, bottle caps

Important: Make sure the plastics are clean and dry to ensure they can be recycled.

2

Find a Collection Point

Locate a participating PlastiRewards collection point near you. Use our map to find the closest location.

3

Drop Off Your Plastics

Bring your collected plastics to the designated collection point and follow the instructions for depositing them.

4

Earn PlastiRewards Tokens

Once your plastics are verified, you'll receive PlastiRewards tokens in your digital wallet. You can then use these tokens to pay for utility bills or other rewards.

Recycling Tips and Guidelines

  • Always clean and dry your plastics before recycling.
  • Remove any labels or caps from the plastic containers.
  • Check with your local collection point for specific requirements.
  • Reduce your plastic consumption whenever possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of plastic are accepted?

We currently accept PET (#1), HDPE (#2), and PP (#5) plastics. Check back for updates as we expand our accepted materials.
